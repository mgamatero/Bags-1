My Story

I was looking to resell some of my designer handbags and couldn't find a reputable outlet online. I decided to design my own website so that designer bag lovers from all over the world could have a site to purchase and sell their bags. In May 2018, Posh Bags officially launched and after just six months has had over a million unique visitors.
